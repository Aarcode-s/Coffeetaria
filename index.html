About Us

Welcome to Coffeetaria

At Coffeetaria, we believe coffee isn’t just a drink — it’s a moment, a mood, a memory.

Nestled in the heart of the city, Coffeetaria is your cozy escape from the rush. Whether you're sipping on a velvety latte, biting into a warm croissant, or catching up with friends, every visit here feels like home.

Our beans are handpicked, our brews are crafted with love, and our snacks are made fresh daily — because you deserve nothing but the best.

Join us for a quick sip, a long chat, or just to soak in the aroma of something good.

"More than a drink — it's a pause, a breath, a beautiful moment. That’s coffee at Coffeetaria." ~ Aaryan.
